Klopp steps down as Liverpool boss!
Jurgen Klopp shocked everyone yesterday. The German coach confirmed that he will leave Liverpool at the end of the season and after 9 years, and will not accept the leadership of any national team or club until the summer of 2025. Klopp also emphasized that he will not manage any team in England other than Liverpool, even if he is starving. In this way, the future of the club will be outside of England.
