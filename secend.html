Having a healthy lifestyle

A healthy lifestyle includes proper nutrition, regular exercise, adequate sleep, stress reduction, positive relationships, and proper recreation. This lifestyle can help as a way to prevent chronic diseases and improve the quality of life. Factors such as eating healthy and fresh food, consuming enough water, using foods with the lowest amount of chemicals, maintaining a balance between exercise and rest, reducing alcohol and drug use, creating positive social relationships and having fun, improving sleep quality and managing stress. They have a great impact on a person's health and psychology and can help increase the quality of life.
Healthy eating: eating healthy and fresh food, eating enough fruits and vegetables, eating healthy and low-fat foods, reducing the consumption of sugar and sweeteners, eating foods with enough nutrients, and eating foods that contain antioxidants. Having a healthy and balanced diet.
